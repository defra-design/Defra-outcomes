# Defra Outcomes Framework

This file is a **readable summary** of the framework. The HTML prototype reads **`app/data/outcomes.json`**. **`reference-defra-outcomes-framework.md`** holds the **full owner names, roles, joint owners, EIP flags, and tables** aligned with that JSON—use it as the complete reference.

---

## Defra vision

**Restoring nature, supporting growth, enhancing security**

As the steward of our natural resources, we strengthen the foundations of our economy and our national security.

---
